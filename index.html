Pronounced [Hahn soom], a word that encompasses Keren's being and by extension, art - Not soley because of its foreign (Korean) & exotic origins, which would be fair given her anthropologic nature and love for different cultures, but also because the word means "breath" or "sigh." Her work is as a deep breath/sigh; a pause button in life's hamster wheel if you will. It explores both dry and wet mediums; from graphite, charcoal, and chalk pastel to ink, acrylic, and oil paint. She hopes to incorporate in her future projects, more 3-dimensional work including but not limited to: wood carving, ceramics, and embroidery.

About the Artist

Keren Nsoyori was born 1998 in Bamenda, English Cameroon, into a culture deeply rooted in community and people. Growing up, she picked up those traits and today, her work in art mostly portrays community and people. She currently lives in Germantown, Maryland where she has created most of her recent works. One prominent theme in Keren's life has been organized progress, whether big or small - She believes in taking the time needed to produce quality pieces & has never rushed her process. She is a dreamer at the core, but also understands the necessity of hard work and due process..
